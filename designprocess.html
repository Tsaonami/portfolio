Design Process

July 14, 2017

The most important tool I used is probably the rectangle tool. I used it to create the boundaries around the different sections to my bakery website design. The second most useful tool, if it's even a tool at all as it is mostly a setting, is the snap to point and snap to guide tool. It helped me align all my pictures, texts, and lines with perfect precision. An obviously useful tool is the text tool, as you need it to type text. The last important tool I used was the sizing tool. It helped me size not only pictures, but text as well. Here is my draft I did in Adobe Illustrator!
